Bsc.Nursing - Academic Eligibility

As per Academic Regulations of course in force from time to time.

  • Candidates who have passed Higher Secondary Examination of the Board of Higher Secondary Education, Kerala, or examinations recognized equivalent thereto by KUHS with 50% in aggregate for Physics, Chemistry and Biology/ Physics, Chemistry, Biology and minimum pass mark as per respective boards for Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English in the qualifying examination.
  • Candidate who completes 17 years of age as on 31st December of the year of admission
  • Candidate shall be medically fit to undergo the course.
  • As per the Regulations published by Kerala University of Health Sciences (KUHS) from time to time.
  • Relaxation of marks for SC/ST and SEBC candidates as per Govt. Norms.
